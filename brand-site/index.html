04 — Voice

Direct. Confident. Technical.

Three constants. No marketing fog, no hedging that hides a weak claim. State verified things plainly, and frame Ezoic as revenue infrastructure — not a generic vendor, ad network, or plugin.

01

Direct

Say what you mean. Cut the fog. If a claim is weak, don't dress it up — sharpen it or drop it.

05 — Usage

What the system is — and isn't.

The brand reads as editorial, technical, and restrained. These guardrails keep every page, deck, and one-pager on-system without copying the homepage blindly.

✓ Do

  • Lead with one dominant idea per section and plenty of negative space.
  • Use approved logos, marks, and assets from the live catalog.
  • Let off-white and near-black carry it; spend green where it counts.
  • Set rounded green pill primary actions with near-black text.
  • Reserve mono for code, hex, file paths, and exact values.
  • Leave metric and quote slots open until a cited value exists.

✕ Don't

  • Fake logos, lockups, or unapproved stand-ins presented as real.
  • All-caps heading or eyebrow systems outside narrow technical use.
  • Generic SaaS card grids, heavy cards, or dashboard layouts as the default.
  • Standalone gradient-blur blobs or grid-pattern filler as "atmosphere."
  • Decorative monospace or mono-heavy layouts.
  • Unsourced metrics, quotes, customers, or invented product claims.
