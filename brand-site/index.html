05System guardrails

What the system is —
and isn't.

Editorial, technical, restrained. These guardrails keep every page, deck, and one-pager on-system without copying the homepage blindly.

＋ Do

  • Lead with one dominant idea per section and generous negative space.
  • Use approved logos, marks, and assets from the live catalog.
  • Let off-white and near-black carry it; spend green where it counts.
  • Reserve mono for code, hex, file paths, and exact values.
  • Leave metric and quote slots open until a cited value exists.

－ Don't

  • Fake logos, lockups, or unapproved stand-ins presented as real.
  • All-caps heading or eyebrow systems outside narrow technical use.
  • Generic SaaS card grids, heavy cards, or dashboard layouts as default.
  • Gradient-blur blobs or grid-pattern filler as “atmosphere.”
  • Unsourced metrics, quotes, customers, or invented product claims.
