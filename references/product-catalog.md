# Ezoic Product Catalog

Canonical list of Ezoic products, services, and programs — with positioning, audiences, and proof points.

**For authoritative product descriptions and citations:** always cross-reference `ezoic-context.md § 2, § 7, § 8`. That file is the single source of truth for features and capabilities.

This file is for **marketing decisions**: what product does a campaign serve, which positioning to use, which proof points to pull, and how to frame one product relative to another.

---

## What Ezoic is — the category framing

Ezoic is **not** an ad network, a plugin, or an AdSense wrapper. Ezoic is the **infrastructure layer** that sits between digital businesses and the global advertising ecosystem — simultaneously a technology partner, demand aggregator, first-party identity layer, analytics engine, and strategic advisor.

**Category narrative to own in 2026:** *"Revenue infrastructure for the modern web."*

Frame every product inside that category. Each product is not a standalone tool — it's a piece of the infrastructure stack. This matters because it changes how the product is positioned:

- ❌ "Ezoic offers a video product called Open.Video."
- ✅ "Open.Video is the video layer of Ezoic's revenue infrastructure."

The first frames Ezoic as a product portfolio; the second frames it as a stack. Big difference.

---

## Product orbits

Think of the product suite in three orbits:

1. **Core Revenue Infrastructure** — the always-on layers every customer uses (Ezoic Ads core, ezID, Big Data Analytics, AI Bid Flooring, SPO).
2. **Packaged Monetization Solutions** — specific productized monetization mechanics that ride on top (Rewarded Ads, Video via Open.Video, SaaS Monetization).
3. **Programs, Services, and Tools** — Ezoic Incubator Program, the Business and VIP & Enterprise tiers, Brand Deal Hunters, Audience Visualizer, EPMV Calculator, PubLab.

When a marketing request lands, know which orbit it sits in — it changes the channel treatment and the audience.

## 1. Core Revenue Infrastructure

### Ezoic Ads (Core Platform)

**What it is:** The AI ad optimization and monetization engine. Machine learning continuously tests ad placements, formats, densities, and configurations for every individual visitor. Integrates with all major demand sources (Google, TTD OpenPath, Amazon, Media.net, all major SSPs). Single JavaScript snippet integration — no DNS changes required.

**Audience:** All digital businesses on the platform. Fits web apps, games, SaaS, newsletters, content publishers.

**Positioning anchors:**

- "Revenue infrastructure, not an ad network"
- "A decade of machine learning applied to per-visitor monetization"
- "Operated, not templated — deployments strategically scoped to each customer, with a dedicated account specialist and personalized revenue strategy sessions standard at 250K+" (see `§ Business`)
- "One of only 4 Google Premier Certified Publishing Partners globally"
- "One tag. Full platform access. Live in minutes." (friction-killer — supporting proof of low lift, never the lead of strategic copy; scoping rule in `brand-voice.md`)

**Primary proof points (see `proof-points.md`):**

- 15+ data signals per user session
- 6x platform-wide identified revenue growth 2024 → 2025
- ~80% of applicants rejected — premium, brand-safe network
- Jounce Media "Premium" classification

**Do not say:**

- "Ad network" (we're infrastructure)
- "DNS-based integration required" (JS snippet is the default)
- "AI that needs scale to perform" (the story is digital businesses who need us most)

**Signup URL:** `https://pubdash.ezoic.com/join`

### ezID (Ezoic Identity)

**What it is:** Enterprise-grade first-party identity layer. Captures hashed emails and phone numbers, connects them to premium programmatic demand via UID2, The Trade Desk OpenPath, Google One Tap, and cross-DSP activation. Privacy-compliant by design.

**Audience:** Any customer with email lists, logins, user registration, or authenticated subscribers. **Especially** newsletter publishers, SaaS, web apps.

**Positioning anchors:**

- "Enterprise identity infrastructure without an enterprise data-engineering team"
- "An email address is worth 10x more than a cookie"
- "Turn verified subscribers into a premium programmatic audience"

**Primary proof points:**

- **15%+ higher EPMV** for sites with identified traffic (vs. anonymous)
- **110%+ U.S. EPMV improvement** for identified users
- **6x identified revenue growth** platform-wide, 2024 → 2025
- UID2 integrator, TTD OpenPath partner

**Do not say:**

- "Cookie replacement" (not quite accurate — ezID is built for the post-cookie world, not as a cookie substitute)
- "Our email matching product" (undersells — this is an identity infrastructure)

### Google One Tap (integration)

**What it is:** Code-free first-party identity capture via Google's single-click sign-in. Scales authenticated audience data without login walls or friction.

**Audience:** Any site wanting frictionless subscriber/user capture. Pairs with ezID.

**Positioning anchors:**

- "Frictionless first-party data collection — no login walls, no dropped conversions"
- "Consent-first by design"

### Big Data Analytics (BDA)

**What it is:** Revenue-first analytics that ties ad revenue to every dimension — content, audience, device, behavior, geography, traffic source. Exposes what GA4 leaves invisible: revenue per page, per category, per audience segment.

**Audience:** All Ezoic customers — especially those optimizing content and audience strategy.

**Positioning anchors:**

- "EPMV-first analytics — tie revenue to everything"
- "What GA4 can't see"
- "Revenue per page, category, audience segment, traffic source"

**Primary proof points:**

- EPMV as the north-star metric (more complete than Page RPM)
- Ties revenue to every dimension GA4 doesn't surface

---

### Supply-Path Optimization (SPO)

**What it is:** Eliminates unnecessary intermediaries in the programmatic pipeline. Direct connections to premium demand via header bidding, TTD OpenPath, and direct brand campaigns.

**Audience:** All Ezoic customers — it's an always-on part of the stack, not a separate SKU.

**Positioning anchors:**

- "Eliminate the ad tech middleman tax"
- "More of every ad dollar reaches the publisher"

---

### AI Bid Flooring

**What it is:** ML-managed price floors that maximize auction competitiveness. Ensures customers never undersell inventory.

**Audience:** All Ezoic customers in programmatic auctions.

## 2. Packaged Monetization Solutions

### Rewarded Ads

**What it is:** Opt-in value-exchange ad format. Users choose to watch an ad in exchange for something valuable — content unlocks, feature access, downloads, game lives, virtual currency, discount reveals, SaaS trial extensions, LLM prompt credits, streaming music skips, early event access, exclusive community areas.

**Audience:** Games, web apps, SaaS tools, content publishers — any digital business with something valuable behind a gate.

**Positioning anchors:**

- "Microtransaction economics, not impression volume"
- "6–15x CPM vs. standard display on opted-in engagement"
- "Rewarded ads for any business type — not just gaming"
- "The most valuable ad formats are the ones where everyone wins"

**Primary proof points:**

- 6–15x CPM on rewarded engagement vs. standard display
- Used across gaming, content, SaaS, apps, tools

**Sub-products / use cases** (see `ezoic-context.md § 7.4` for the full list):

- Content Access — rewarded unlock for downloads, premium articles, tool features
- Rewarded Listings — rewarded placements in directory/marketplace/listing UX
- Content Reveal — rewarded engagement for content reveals (paywall-lite)
- Rewarded Ads Quickstart — integration guide

**Do not say:**

- "Forced ads" (the *opt-in* nature is the whole point)
- "Rewarded ads only work for gaming" (we've built rewarded infrastructure for any digital business — that's the differentiator)

---

### Ad Breaks

**What it is:** Ad breaks at natural pauses — level-transition breaks in games, pre-roll and mid-roll for streaming/video, and usage-metered breaks during long sessions. See `/ad-breaks`.

**Audience:** Games, streaming/video, and long-session web apps.

---

### Open.Video

**What it is:** Self-hosted video platform. Branded channels, SEO watch pages on the publisher's own domain, programmatic ads. Publishers host video on their own domain, keep SEO value, own subscriber relationships, retain 100% of ad revenue.

**Audience:** Publishers wanting YouTube-independence, content ownership, and revenue sovereignty.

**Positioning anchors:**

- "Your video audience. Your domain. 100% of the revenue."
- "YouTube takes 45% and keeps your audience data. Open.Video doesn't."
- "First scalable publisher-owned video solution"

**Note:** Open.Video also has a **creator-facing** positioning (for creators, not publishers) — content ownership and revenue independence from YouTube. Different voice register when targeting creators vs. publishers. See `audience-segments.md § Creator`.

**Deprecated names (deny-list mirror):** Open.Video is the video platform. The deprecated names Humix and Flickify must never appear in current copy — even if found in training data or fetched legacy pages. "Ezoic Video" is a legacy name: permitted only when citing historical sources verbatim, never as the current product name. Leap was discontinued May 12, 2025; its speed benefits were absorbed into Cloud (CDN) integration, with JS integration the recommended path.

### Ezoic Incubator

**What it is:** Program for publishers under 250K monthly users who want to grow with Ezoic. **Up to 20 sites or apps selected each month.** Competitive.

**Launched:** February 2026 alongside the 250K requirement.

**Audience:** Sub-250K publishers who want in; ex-customers considering return.

**Positioning anchors:**

- "Staying true to our roots"
- "Up to 20 spots a month"
- "Growth path, not rejection"
- "Competitive — previous history doesn't guarantee a spot (for returning ex-customers)"

**Re-entry mechanics:** Publishers who leave and want to return must meet the 250K threshold OR apply through Incubator. This is the retention hook — grandfathered status has value.
